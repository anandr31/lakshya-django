About Internship Funding

Why should I sponsor a student's internship program?

Internships are a proven way to gain relevant knowledge, skills, and experience while building an important network of experts in the field. It makes the classroomâ€™s theories and teaching more relevant and incrases retention through practical research and real-world applications. Not every university offering an internship will provide a stipend which is immensely necessary for students to sustain themselves during the internhip period.

How will it impact the NITW ecosystem?

  • Confidence: The financial back up will enable students to apply for prestigious universities in India and abroad. Getting an internship in top colleges is a challenging task for which requires research bent and aptitude.
  • Awareness: The students who benefit through this program conduct sessions for junior batches and guide them on various aspects of building a profile, applying for an internship, and on what to expect. We began with 5 applications in 2013 and 15 in 2014. This clearly indicates the growing interest and capability of our students.

How are funds transferred to the student?

A cheque is made out and given to each beneficiary. Once the donation is made, you will receive a conformation mail and a receipt which can be used to claim tax exemption.
