Where your donation goes

Vision Development Programs
  • Scholarships: Various chapter and batches pledge to support students under our interest-free loan model. We finance students' institute fee which is repaid within five years of the student's graduation. Every single scholar who has graduated has started repaying and some have given over and above what they received.

  • Research, Entrepreneurship and Creativity Development: We have an MoU with NIT Warangal to support and fund research and innovation by students and also sponsor innovative and research opportunities outside the MoU. We conduct events to creat non-credit learning opportunities like workshops, guest-lectures, networking events, competitions etc.
People and technology:
    We have 2 employees and hire interns from time to time to work on achieving our vision. We also develop technology to make the NIT Warangal administration more efficient. The Alumni Request Automation and Bills and Advances Tracking System are two such examples. Funds are also used to keep web servers running and for office space rentals.
